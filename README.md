## 🚀 Vision

**We are building the operating layer for AI-driven real estate, agritech, and urban innovation.**

blu|mark Studio is a Grand Rapids-based digital innovation studio developing production-ready AI tools and open data platforms across three core domains. Each project is designed to close the gap between institutional-grade analysis and accessible, community-focused outcomes — from acquisition underwriting to controlled-environment agriculture and workforce-aligned design.

This repository serves as the **public portfolio hub and digital home base** for all projects developed under the blu|mark brand, providing transparent access to tools, code, and project documentation for collaborators, investors, and community stakeholders.

---

## 📍 Why Grand Rapids?

Grand Rapids, Michigan is an emerging hub for real estate innovation and urban agritech:

- **Real Estate Market:** A rapidly evolving mid-market city with significant acquisition and redevelopment opportunity across residential and commercial sectors.
- **Water & Infrastructure:** Great Lakes basin access positions the region for climate-resilient food systems and sustainable development.
- **Institutional Density:** Proximity to Michigan State University AgBioResearch, GVSU, and the Grand Rapids SmartZone drives data partnership potential.
- **Workforce & Innovation:** A growing tech and creative economy with active community development corridors ideal for workforce-integrated AI deployments.
